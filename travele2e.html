Travel Analytics

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the Argo Datathon 2019 Travel Dataset from Kaggle to uncover insights that can improve travel recommendations and services. By leveraging data, I aim to answer critical business questions and enhance the overall travel experience.

Why I Chose This Project

As a passionate traveler, I always seek new experiences and insights into customer preferences. Travel is more than just visiting places; itâ€™s about understanding how to optimize services and enhance user experiences. This inspired me to analyze the Argo Datathon 2019 dataset to uncover actionable insights that can improve travel recommendations and services.

The key business question I aimed to answer was: "How can Argo offer the best travel experience for its customers?" By analyzing travel patterns, user behavior, and service preferences, I provided data-driven recommendations to improve customer satisfaction and engagement.

What I Learned

Through this project, I gained valuable experience in:

Data Warehousing

  • Extracting raw travel data from Kaggle and transforming it by handling missing values, standardizing formats, and enriching it with calculated fields.
  • Loading (ETL process) the refined data into a structured format for analysis.

Reports

After cleaning the data and performing advanced analytics, I created three key reports to provide actionable insights for stakeholders:

  • Users Report:
    • Segments users into VIP, Regular, and New categories.
    • Aggregates metrics: total bookings, purchases, and lifespan.
    • Calculates KPIs: recency, average order value, and monthly spend.
